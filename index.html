F. A. Q.

How do I start recording?
Simply tap the REC button on the main screen. The app will immediately begin capturing audio until you press REC button again. Recordings are saved in list.
Where are my recordings saved?
Recordings are automatically stored in the list in screen. You can access them one time while page is open.
Does the app work offline?
Absolutely. You won’t need an internet connection to Record your audios. Just keep page open.
What audio format are recordings saved in?
Recordings are saved in .webm format (or another format depending on your navigator), ensuring compatibility with most navigators and devices.
